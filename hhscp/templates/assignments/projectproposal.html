Once you have completed, at minimum, the Conways Life project, you have the option of continuing with the game trees projects or defining your own project.

Before you can begin working on an independent project, you must e-mail, share or hand a proposal document to me.

Your Proposal

Your proposal must answer the following questions:

  • What is the name of your project?
  • Who will be working on the project (you and optionally one partner). Partner projects may submit ONE proposal for both.
  • Describe what the result of your project will look like, or how it will work, from the perspective of someone using it.
  • Describe how your project will work, from the point of view of the programmer.
  • How will you and I know when your project is finished. Please be specific! The project must have hard, measurable performance goals that can be verified.
  • What do you (and your partner) need to learn in order to finish the project?
  • How many weeks do you think it will take to finish the project?

Project Idea

Consider writing your own customized client for the moderately Multiplayer Online Space Shooter, mMOSS. You can download the Python source code for version 0.0.4 and try it out with PyCharm. There is a mMOSS server running on netdenizen.org that you can test against.
